HOW TO ORDER




Use the CONTACT page and submit the following:

#1 What you want to purchase and the quantity (10 items MAX per transaction!)

#2 Your delivery location for shipping!

#3 Wait for a confirmation email purchase order with invoice number and make payment
all payments are to be in BTC (bitcoin) no other payment method will be accepted!

#5 Once payment has been approved you will receive a reply email with transaction documentation!

#6 Once items have been shipped (usually same or next day) you will be provided with a tracking number for your delivery!

FYI: shipping within australia is a flat rate of $15 AUD
shipping outside australia is a flat rate of $40 AUD
